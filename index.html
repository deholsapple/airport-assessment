Always verify safety-critical fields against the current FAA Chart Supplement and NOTAMs before dispatch.
CRITERIA NOT VERIFIED One or more thresholds in criteria.json have not been reviewed against the AFM / ops specs. Every criterion depending on an unset threshold reads NOT EVALUATED in the generated report. Do not use for dispatch.
